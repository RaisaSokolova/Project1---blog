Relaxing is the way to happiness!

March 25, 2023

Well, my readers, today we will talk about how to relax properly. The first thing to say is the place of rest.
It must be equipped with everything necessary:

  • there should be enough space so that you can lie down to your full height along with the tail;
  • should be soft or hard (depending on preferences);
  • should be warm or cool (depending on the weather and the length of the coat);
  • there should be comfortable beds;
  • favorite places should be free (for example, a cardboard box or a place on a wardrobe).

If you have problems with the above, you need to urgently inform the one who gives us food and who cleans our toilet.
If there is still no understanding, I suggest moving on to waking up this person at night by running, screaming or jumping on him.
